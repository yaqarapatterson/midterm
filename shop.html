Shopping & Food

Downtown Crossing


A popular shopping district located conveniently near MBTA station, visit Downtown Crossing to go to a multitude of stores and restaurants and for an easy walk to Boston Common or the Financial District

Downtown Boston Website

Newbury Street


An endless street of stores and places to eat. Newbury Street is a popular destination in the warm spring months, located in Back Bay, Boston, stretching a mile long.

Newbury Street Website

Prudential Center


The Prudential Center in Back Bay, Boston is an urban shopping mall located at the base of the Prudential Tower. other place to shop and dine. A great option for an indoor experience, despite the weather. It features over 75 shops and restaurants.

Visit Prudential Center's official Website for more information
